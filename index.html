🧶 Our Mission

At Desi Kaarigari, we believe that every handmade ornament carries a spark of India’s cultural soul. We aim to:

  • Promote traditional Indian jewellery crafts (like Meenakari, Lac, Filigree)
  • Empower local women artisans by helping them earn and grow
  • Keep Indian handwork alive by connecting it to new audiences

This is a commerce-for-impact platform — small in scale but rich in heart.
